Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Inspiration of billion lives

Missile man of India

Here are some of remarkable achievements and facts about Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam:

  • Dr APJ Abdul Kalam oversaw multiple nuclear testing at Pokhran. Dr. Kalam was the then-Prime Minister's Chief Scientific Adviser and oversaw the nuclear tests at Pokhran-II.
  • Dr. Kalam directed the nuclear tests that turned India into a nuclear power from July 1992 to December 1999.
  • Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam led India's two largest science research organizations, the Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO) and the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO).
  • Dr. Kalam is credited with creating and operating the 'Indigenous Guided Missiles' (AGNI and PRITHVI).
  • Dr. Kalam spent more than ten years at ISRO as the project director for the development of the indigenous Satellite Launch Vehicle.
  • Under the direction of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, India's SLV-III successfully launched the Rohini satellite into near-Earth orbit in July 1980, making India a member of the exclusive space club.
